Chicken Karahi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 4-5 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a karahi or deep pan over medium heat. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add chicken pieces to the pan and cook on high heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook for 20-25 minutes until tender. Stir occasionally.
  7. Add chopped green chilies and garam masala. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

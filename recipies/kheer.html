Kheer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews)
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak it for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring the milk to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Add the soaked and drained rice to the simmering milk. Cook on low heat, stirring frequently, until the rice is soft and the mixture thickens (about 45 minutes).
  4. Add sugar, chopped nuts, raisins, cardamom powder, and saffron strands (if using). Stir well and cook for another 10-15 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Kheer can be served warm or chilled.

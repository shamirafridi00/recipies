Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cups water
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add chicken pieces to the pot and cook until browned.
  4. Add yogurt, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  5. In another pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain.
  6. Layer the chicken mixture and rice in a large pot. Sprinkle with fried onions, cilantro, and mint.
  7. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
